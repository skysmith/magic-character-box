Story album

Photo stories

Pair an NFC sticker with a printed photo, create a phone recording link, and let the box play that story whenever the photo is tapped.

  1. Scan the sticker before it goes on the photo.
  2. Name the photo or memory.
  3. Open the recorder link on a phone and save the story.
  4. Stick the tag to the back of the photo.

Family messages

Guest recording links

Create a private upload-only page for a family member. They can record or upload one message from their browser, and it saves straight into the selected character.

  1. Create a link for the right character.
  2. Leave the external URL blank for someone on your Wi-Fi.
  3. Paste a guest-only HTTPS tunnel URL for remote family.
  4. Send the guest link or print its QR, not the admin dashboard.

The dashboard fills this with the best secure address it can see. Use the Tailscale address for your devices, or paste a public guest-only tunnel URL for someone outside your tailnet.

3D figure notes

Prompt shape

"A small friendly rocket mascot toy, simple rounded shapes, flat circular base, no thin parts, designed for FDM 3D printing, single solid object."

NFC base

Use a 35-45 mm wide base. Add a shallow underside recess for a 25-30 mm NFC sticker, then cover it with felt or tape.

Print check

Inspect in a slicer before printing. Avoid skinny arms, sharp spikes, fragile antennas, and unsupported overhangs.

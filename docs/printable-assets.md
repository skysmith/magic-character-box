# Printable Assets

These simple printables make the project feel like a gift instead of a loose electronics demo.

## Top Label

Print or hand-letter this on the lid:

```text
Place Character Here
```

Put the PN532 reader directly under this spot. Avoid metal foil labels.

## NFC Sticker Note

If you print labels for the underside of figures, keep them thin. A thick stack of plastic, foam tape, or label material increases the distance between the NFC sticker and the reader.

## Open Source Photo Checklist

For public photos, use:

- Generic toy dinosaurs, rockets, robots, animals, or printed shapes.
- Your own labels and art.
- The printed NFC base from [3d-printable-figures.md](3d-printable-figures.md).
- No copyrighted music, private recordings, or branded toy characters in the repo.

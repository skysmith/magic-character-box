# Birthday Mission Cards

## Your Magic Character Box

These characters are empty right now.
You get to choose what powers they have.
Place one on top of the box.
The box will learn who it is.
Then we will teach it what to play.

## Mission 1: Wake The Box

Turn it on and hear the startup sound.

## Mission 2: Teach A Character

Scan a new character, name it, and add its first sound.

## Mission 3: Make A Secret Dad Token

Record a special message.

## Printable Copy

See [printable-assets.md](printable-assets.md) for label and card text you can print or paste into a document.

## Mission 4: Build The Real Case

Decorate the box and make it yours.

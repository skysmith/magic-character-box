# Audio Sources

The repo does not include audio files. Add your own `.mp3` files under the character folders:

```text
audio/dinosaur/
audio/rocket/
audio/dad/
```

## Good Sources

- Family voice messages.
- Original recordings.
- Public-domain stories or music.
- Creative Commons audio when the license allows your use.
- Legally owned personal files for private, local playback where allowed.

## Avoid

- Committing copyrighted songs or audiobooks.
- Publishing private family recordings.
- Treating NFC tags as access control or security.
- Depending on streaming services for the first build.

## Starter Ideas

- Dinosaur: roar, dino fact, stomp sound.
- Rocket: countdown, launch sound, planet fact.
- Dad Token: goodnight message, birthday message, secret mission.
- Mystery Token: one random silly sound from a local folder.
